# ZaiGit Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

The ZaiGit community is committed to providing a respectful, inclusive, and constructive environment for everyone, regardless of experience, identity, background, or level of participation.

## Expected Conduct

Community members are expected to:

* Communicate respectfully and constructively.
* Provide clear, actionable feedback.
* Focus discussion on ideas, behavior, and technical outcomes.
* Welcome questions and support new contributors.
* Acknowledge the work and ideas of others.
* Respect privacy and confidentiality.
* Accept responsibility for mistakes and make reasonable efforts to correct them.

## Unacceptable Conduct

The following behavior is not acceptable:

* Harassment, intimidation, discrimination, or abusive language
* Personal attacks or deliberately inflammatory comments
* Publishing private information without permission
* Unwanted sexual attention or inappropriate imagery
* Repeated disruption, spam, trolling, or deliberate derailment
* Retaliation against a person who reports a concern in good faith
* Conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional community

## Scope

This code applies in project spaces and whenever an individual represents the project or ZaiCode Labs in public. Project spaces include issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, community events, websites, and official communication channels.

## Reporting and Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to [info@zaicodelabs.co.za](mailto:info@zaicodelabs.co.za). Include relevant links, dates, context, and supporting material when possible.

Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as circumstances allow. Project maintainers may remove content, issue warnings, restrict participation, or permanently exclude individuals whose conduct violates this policy. Enforcement decisions will consider the context, severity, frequency, and impact of the behavior.

Retaliation against anyone who submits or assists with a good faith report is prohibited.

## Questions

Questions about this policy may be sent to [info@zaicodelabs.co.za](mailto:info@zaicodelabs.co.za).
